Chess Variation

Here's the rules:

  • As in standard chess, white moves first.
  • The first player to move their king onto row 8 is the winner.
  • Pieces move and capture the same as in standard chess.
  • As in standard chess, a player is not allowed to expose their own king to check (including moving a piece that was blocking a check such that it no longer does).
  • Unlike standard chess, a player is not allowed to put the opponent's king in check (including moving a piece that was blocking a check such that it no longer does).
